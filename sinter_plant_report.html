This process involves key parameters such as temperature, pressure, material feed rates, air flow, and fuel rates, which are represented in the dataset as anonymized variables X1 to X14. These parameters influence stack emissions (the output), a critical metric for environmental compliance, typically measured as particulate matter or gaseous pollutants like CO₂, SO₂, or NOx. The dataset, spanning December 10–31, 2022, with 2,461 records collected at 10-minute intervals, provides a comprehensive basis for analyzing emission trends and predicting future values.
